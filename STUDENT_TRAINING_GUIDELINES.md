## HOW TO LOGIN

### Step 1: Open Your Browser

1. Open your internet browser (Chrome, Safari, etc.)
2. Type or click the school portal address
3. Press Enter

### Step 2: Find the Login Page

You'll see a page with:
- School name and logo
- Two boxes to type in
- A "Login" button

### Step 3: Enter Your Details

**Email Box:**
- Type your school email address
- Example: `5@student.com` or `charles.mwaba@student.stfrancisofassisizm.com`
- Be careful with spelling!

**Password Box:**
- Type: `student.1234`
- This is your first-time password
- You'll change it later for security

### Step 4: Click Login

- Click the big **"Login"** button
- Or press **Enter** on your keyboard

### Step 5: Change Your Password (First Time Only)

**WHY?** To keep your account safe!

The system will ask you to create a new password:

1. **Current Password:** Type `student.1234`
2. **New Password:** Create your own password
   - At least 8 letters/numbers
   - Use CAPITAL and small letters
   - Add numbers (1, 2, 3)
   - Add symbols (@, #, $, !)
   - Example: `MySchool2025!`

3. **Confirm New Password:** Type the same password again
4. Click **"Change Password"**

**REMEMBER YOUR PASSWORD!** Write it down somewhere safe at home.

### Step 6: You're In!

🎉 Congratulations! You should now see your dashboard with:
- Your name at the top
- Menu options on the side or top
- Boxes showing your information

## STEP-BY-STEP GUIDES

### GUIDE 1: Viewing Your Homework

**Time Needed:** 2 minutes

**Steps:**

1. **Go to Homework Section**
   - Look for **"Homework"** or **"Assignments"** in the menu
   - Click on it

2. **See Your Homework List**
   You'll see:
   - Subject (Math, English, etc.)
   - Assignment title
   - Teacher who gave it
   - Due date
   - Status (Pending/Submitted)

3. **Open an Assignment**
   - Click on any homework to see full details
   - Read the instructions carefully
   - Check what you need to do
   - Note the due date

4. **Download Materials**
   - If teacher attached files, you'll see **"Download"** button
   - Click to download worksheets or resources
   - Save to your device

5. **Check Due Date**
   - Look for the deadline
   - Note how many days you have left
   - Plan when you'll do it

---

### GUIDE 2: Submitting Homework Online

**Time Needed:** 5 minutes

**Steps:**

1. **Complete Your Homework**
   - Do your homework on paper or computer
   - Check it's complete and correct
   - If on paper, take clear photos or scan it

2. **Go to Assignment**
   - Click **"Homework"**
   - Find the assignment to submit
   - Click on it to open

3. **Click Submit**
   - Look for **"Submit Assignment"** button
   - Click it

4. **Upload Your Work**
   - Click **"Choose File"** or **"Upload"**
   - Find your file on your device
   - Select it
   - Acceptable formats:
     - PDF files (.pdf)
     - Word documents (.doc, .docx)
     - Images (.jpg, .png)
     - Maximum size: 5MB

5. **Add Comments (Optional)**
   - You can add a note to your teacher
   - Explain anything special about your work

6. **Submit**
   - Double-check you uploaded the right file
   - Click **"Submit"**
   - You'll see a confirmation message

7. **Confirmation**
   - Assignment status changes to "Submitted"
   - Teacher receives notification
   - You can see submission time and date

**⚠️ Important:**
- Submit BEFORE the due date
- Check file uploaded correctly
- Keep a copy of your work
- Can't change after submitting (ask teacher if you need to)

---

### GUIDE 3: Checking Your Grades

**Time Needed:** 3 minutes

**Steps:**

1. **Open Grades Section**
   - Click **"Grades"** or **"Results"** in menu
   - You'll see list of subjects

2. **View by Subject**
   - Click on any subject to see details
   - You'll see:
     - Test names
     - Dates
     - Your score
     - Maximum marks
     - Percentage
     - Grade (A, B, C, etc.)

3. **Read Teacher Comments**
   - Click on any grade
   - Teacher may have added comments
   - Read feedback carefully
   - Use it to improve

4. **View Overall Grade**
   - See your average for each subject
   - Check your overall performance
   - Compare with previous terms

5. **View Report Card**
   - Click **"Report Card"** or **"Download Report"**
   - See your full report
   - Print or save as PDF

**Understanding Your Grades:**
- **A (90-100%):** Excellent! Outstanding work
- **B (80-89%):** Very Good! Keep it up
- **C (70-79%):** Good! You're doing well
- **D (60-69%):** Satisfactory! You can improve
- **E (50-59%):** Fair! Need more effort
- **F (Below 50%):** Needs improvement! Ask for help

### GUIDE 5: Checking Attendance

**Time Needed:** 2 minutes

**Steps:**

1. **Go to Attendance**
   - Click **"Attendance"** in menu

2. **View Your Record**
   You'll see:
   - 📅 Dates

   - ❌ Absent days (red)

## TROUBLESHOOTING PROBLEMS

### Problem 1: I Forgot My Password! 😰

**Solution:**
1. Go to login page
2. Click **"Forgot Password?"** link
3. Enter your email address
4. Check your email for reset link
5. Click link and create new password
6. Remember your new password!

**Still Can't Access?**
- Ask your class teacher
- Tell your parents
- Visit school IT office

---

### Problem 2: I Can't See My Homework! 😕

**Solution:**
1. Make sure you're logged in
2. Click **"Homework"** in the menu
3. Try refreshing the page (press F5)
4. Check you're looking at correct class
5. Ask teacher if homework was assigned

**If Nothing Shows:**
- Teacher may not have posted yet
- Check back later
- Ask classmates
- Email teacher

---

### Problem 3: My Upload Won't Work! 📁

**Solution:**
1. Check file size (must be under 5MB)
2. Check file type:
   - ✅ PDF (.pdf)
   - ✅ Word (.doc, .docx)
   - ✅ Images (.jpg, .png)
   - ❌ Videos (too big)
   - ❌ Zip files
3. Try smaller file
4. Check internet connection
5. Try again later
6. Ask parent or teacher for help

**How to Make File Smaller:**
- Compress images before uploading
- Convert to PDF format
- Use online compression tools
- Ask parent for help

---

### Problem 4: Page Won't Load! ⏳

**Solution:**
1. Check your internet connection
2. Try refreshing (press F5)
3. Close and reopen browser
4. Try different browser
5. Restart your device
6. Try again in a few minutes

**Still Not Working?**
- Internet might be down
- Portal might be under maintenance
- Try later or next day
- Tell teacher if urgent

---

### Problem 5: I Submitted Wrong File! 😱

**Solution:**
1. **Don't panic!**
2. Tell your teacher immediately
3. Send message through portal
4. Or email teacher
5. Explain what happened
6. Ask if you can resubmit

**How to Prevent:**
- Always check file before submitting
- Open file to make sure it's correct
- Name files clearly
- Keep organized folders

## GETTING HELP

### Who Can Help You?

**At School:**
1. **Your Class Teacher**
   - First person to ask
   - Can help with most problems
   - Knows your account details

2. **IT Teacher/Staff**
   - Helps with technical problems
   - Can reset passwords
   - Fixes login issues

3. **School Office**
   - Can contact IT support
   - Has your account information
   - Can help parents contact school

**At Home:**
1. **Your Parents**
   - Help with technical issues
   - Support with homework
   - Contact school if needed

2. **Older Siblings**
   - May have experience with portal
   - Can guide you
   - Help with technology

### How to Ask for Help

**Be Specific:**
❌ "It's not working!"
✅ "I can't login. It says 'incorrect password'"

**Provide Details:**
- What were you trying to do?
- What happened?
- What error message did you see?
- When did it happen?

**Take Screenshots:**
- Press "Print Screen" button
- Paste in document
- Show to teacher or parent
- Helps explain problem

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: Can I access portal from my phone?**
A: Yes! Portal works on phones, tablets, and computers.

**Q: Can I use portal at home?**
A: Yes! Access from anywhere with internet.

**Q: What if I don't have internet at home?**
A: Use school WiFi during breaks or after class. Talk to teacher about options.

**Q: Can my parents see my grades?**
A: Yes, parents have their own login to see your progress.

**Q: What if I submit homework late?**
A: You can still submit, but teacher will see it's late. Talk to teacher about late policy.

**Q: Can I delete a submitted assignment?**
A: No, but you can ask teacher if you need to change something.

**Q: How do I know if teacher read my submission?**
A: Assignment status will update. Teacher may grade it or leave comments.

**Q: What if my grade is wrong?**
A: Talk to your teacher. Mistakes can be corrected.

**Q: Can I print my report card?**
A: Yes! Click print button when viewing report card.

**Q: What if I lose my password?**
A: Use "Forgot Password" link or ask teacher to reset it.
