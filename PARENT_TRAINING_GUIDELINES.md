## HOW TO LOGIN

### Step 1: Access the Portal

**On Computer:**
1. Open your web browser
2. Type the school portal address: **[Insert Portal URL]**
3. Press Enter
4. Bookmark the page for easy access later

**On Phone/Tablet:**
1. Open browser app (Chrome, Safari, etc.)
2. Type the portal address
3. Save to home screen for quick access

### Step 2: Enter Your Credentials

On the login page you'll see two fields:

**Email Address:**
- Type the email address you registered with school
- Example: `johnbanda1@parent.com` or `ben@gmail.com`
- Double-check spelling
- Email is case-sensitive

**Password:**
- For first login, type: `parent.1234`
- This is temporary - you'll change it
- Password is case-sensitive
- Make sure Caps Lock is OFF

### Step 3: Click Login

- Click the **"Login"** button
- Or press **Enter** on keyboard
- System will verify your details

### Step 4: Change Your Password (First Time)

**For Security:** You must create your own password

1. System will prompt you to change password
2. **Current Password:** Type `parent.1234`
3. **New Password:** Create a strong password
   - At least 8 characters
   - Mix of uppercase and lowercase (A-z)
   - Include numbers (0-9)
   - Include symbols (@, #, $, !)
   - Example: `MyChild@School2025`

4. **Confirm Password:** Type new password again
5. Click **"Update Password"**

**Password Tips:**
✅ Use something memorable but not obvious
✅ Don't use child's name alone
✅ Don't use "password" or "123456"
✅ Write it down and keep it secure
✅ Don't share with others

### Step 5: Welcome to Your Dashboard!

After successful login, you'll see:
- Your name displayed
- Your child's/children's information
- Navigation menu
- Quick overview of information

## STEP-BY-STEP GUIDES

### GUIDE 1: Checking Your Child's Latest Grades

**Time Required:** 3-5 minutes

**Steps:**

1. **Login to Portal**
   - Go to portal website
   - Enter email and password
   - Click Login

2. **Navigate to Grades**
   - Look for **"Grades"**, **"Results"**, or **"Academic Progress"**
   - Click on it

3. **Select Your Child**
   - If you have multiple children, select the one you want to view
   - Click their name

4. **Choose Term/Period**
   - Select current term or specific period
   - Choose "All Subjects" or specific subject

5. **Review Grades**
   - See all test scores
   - Check subject grades
   - Read teacher comments
   - Note areas for improvement

6. **Take Action**
   - Discuss results with child
   - Praise good performance
   - Plan support for weak areas
   - Contact teacher if concerned

**Tips:**
- Check grades weekly
- Compare with previous term
- Look for trends (improving/declining)
- Celebrate all achievements
- Address concerns early

---

### GUIDE 2: Monitoring Daily Attendance

**Time Required:** 2 minutes

**Steps:**

1. **Open Attendance Section**
   - Click **"Attendance"** in menu
   - Select your child

2. **Choose View**
   - **Daily:** See today's attendance
   - **Weekly:** See this week
   - **Monthly:** See whole month
   - **Custom:** Choose date range

3. **Review Attendance**
   - Green = Present ✅
   - Red = Absent ❌
   - Yellow = Late ⏰
   - Blue = Excused 📝

4. **Check Summary Stats**
   - Total days: ___
   - Days present: ___
   - Days absent: ___
   - Attendance %: ___

5. **Verify Accuracy**
   - If you see unexpected absence
   - Contact school immediately
   - Records may need correction

6. **Monitor Patterns**
   - Is child absent often on specific days?
   - Too many late arrivals?
   - Attendance dropping?
   - Address issues promptly

**Red Flags:**
- Attendance below 90%
- Multiple consecutive absences
- Frequent late arrivals
- Unexplained absences

---

### GUIDE 3: Reviewing Homework Assignments

**Time Required:** 5 minutes

**Steps:**

1. **Go to Homework Section**
   - Click **"Homework"** or **"Assignments"**
   - Select your child

2. **See Current Assignments**
   - List shows all active homework
   - Sorted by due date
   - Status indicators visible

3. **Check Each Assignment**
   For each homework, note:
   - ✏️ Subject
   - 📋 What needs to be done
   - 📅 When it's due
   - ✅ If child submitted it
   - 💯 Grade (if graded)

4. **Download Materials**
   - If teacher attached worksheets

   - Save or print for child

5. **Create Action Plan**
   - List pending assignments
   - Note due dates
   - Help child prioritize
   - Set up study schedule

6. **Follow Up**
   - Check back after due date
   - Verify submission
   - Review teacher feedback
   - Praise completed work

**Daily Routine:**
- Check homework every afternoon
- Help child plan evening
- Ensure child understands tasks
- Verify completion before bed

---

### GUIDE 4: Sending a Message to Teacher

**Time Required:** 5-10 minutes

**Steps:**

1. **Go to Messages**
   - Click **"Messages"** or **"Communication"**
   - Click **"New Message"** or **"Compose"**

2. **Select Teacher**
   - Choose recipient:
     - Class teacher (general concerns)
     - Subject teacher (specific subject issues)
     - School admin (administrative matters)

3. **Write Subject Line**
   - Be clear and specific
   - Example: "Question about Math Homework"
   - Example: "Request for Parent-Teacher Meeting"

4. **Compose Message**
   - Be polite and respectful
   - State your concern clearly
   - Provide necessary details
   - Suggest solutions if applicable
   - Keep it concise

**Good Message Structure:**
```
Dear [Teacher Name],

I am writing regarding [specific topic/concern].

[Details of your concern or question]

[What you would like to happen/know]

Thank you for your attention to this matter.

Best regards,
[Your Name]
Parent of [Child's Name], [Class]
```

5. **Attach Files (If Needed)**
   - Medical certificates
   - Permission slips
   - Relevant documents

6. **Review and Send**
   - Reread your message
   - Check spelling
   - Ensure clarity
   - Click **"Send"**

7. **Wait for Response**
   - Teachers respond within 24-48 hours
   - Check messages regularly
   - Reply promptly when teacher responds

**When to Message Teacher:**
✅ Questions about homework
✅ Concerns about child's progress
✅ Request for meeting
✅ Explanation for absence
✅ Discuss behavioral issues
✅ Clarify expectations

**When to Call Instead:**
❌ Emergencies
❌ Urgent matters
❌ Same-day issues
❌ Complex discussions

## TROUBLESHOOTING

### Problem 1: Can't Login

**Possible Causes:**
- Incorrect email address
- Wrong password
- Caps Lock is on
- Account not yet created
- Browser issues

**Solutions:**
1. Double-check email spelling
2. Verify password (case-sensitive)
3. Check Caps Lock is OFF
4. Try "Forgot Password" link
5. Clear browser cache and cookies
6. Try different browser
7. Contact school if still unable to login

**Forgot Password:**
1. Click **"Forgot Password?"** on login page
2. Enter your registered email
3. Check email for reset link
4. Click link (valid for 24 hours)
5. Create new password
6. Login with new password

---

### Problem 2: Can't See My Child's Information

**Possible Causes:**
- Child not linked to your account
- Incorrect child selected
- Data not yet uploaded
- Technical issue

**Solutions:**
1. Verify child is in your profile
2. Check correct child is selected
3. Refresh page (F5)
4. Log out and log back in
5. Contact school to verify account setup
6. Ensure child is registered in current term

---

### Problem 3: Grades Not Showing

**Possible Causes:**
- Teacher hasn't posted grades yet
- Grades not yet published
- Looking at wrong term
- Technical delay

**Solutions:**
1. Verify correct term is selected
2. Check with child if test happened recently
3. Teachers may take 1-2 weeks to post grades
4. Refresh page
5. Contact teacher if grades overdue
6. Check announcements for information

---

### Problem 4: Can't Download Report Card

**Possible Causes:**
- Report not yet published
- Pop-up blocker enabled
- File size too large
- Browser doesn't support PDF
- Slow internet connection

**Solutions:**
1. Disable pop-up blocker for portal
2. Try different browser
3. Check internet connection
4. Try downloading at different time
5. Right-click and "Save As"
6. Contact IT support
7. Request printed copy from school

---

### Problem 5: Not Receiving Notifications

**Possible Causes:**
- Notifications disabled
- Wrong email on file
- Emails going to spam
- Settings not saved properly

**Solutions:**
1. Check notification settings
2. Verify email address is correct
3. Check spam/junk folder
4. Add school email to contacts
5. Mark school emails as "Not Spam"
6. Update notification preferences
7. Try different email address
8. Contact school to verify email on file

---

### Problem 6: Portal Running Slowly

**Possible Causes:**
- Slow internet connection
- Too many tabs open
- Browser cache full
- Device memory low
- Peak usage time

**Solutions:**
1. Check internet speed
2. Close unnecessary tabs/programs
3. Clear browser cache
4. Restart browser
5. Restart device
6. Try at different time (early morning/late evening)
7. Use different device
8. Contact IT support if persistent

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### General Questions

**Q: Is there a charge to use the parent portal?**
A: No, the portal is free for all parents.

**Q: Can I access the portal from my phone?**
A: Yes! The portal works on smartphones, tablets, and computers.

**Q: Is there an app I can download?**
A: Currently, access is through web browser. Check with school about future app availability.

**Q: How often is information updated?**
A: Teachers update regularly. Grades typically within 1-2 weeks, attendance daily, homework as assigned.

**Q: What if I don't have internet at home?**
A: Visit school office to use computer, use mobile data, go to library, or ask relative/friend.

**Q: Can both parents have access?**
A: Yes! Each parent can have separate login credentials. Contact school to set up.

**Q: What if I have children in different classes/grades?**
A: One parent account can access multiple children. Switch between them in the portal.

### Technical Questions

**Q: Which browser works best?**
A: Google Chrome is recommended, but Firefox, Safari, and Edge also work well.

**Q: I forgot my password. What should I do?**
A: Use "Forgot Password" link on login page or contact school office.

**Q: Can I change my email address?**
A: Contact school office to update email address in system.

**Q: Is my information secure?**
A: Yes, the portal uses encryption and security measures to protect your data.

**Q: Why does the portal log me out?**
A: For security, portal logs out after period of inactivity (usually 30 minutes).

### Content Questions

**Q: Why don't I see all my child's grades?**
A: Teachers post grades after they're finalized. Some may not be published yet.

**Q: The attendance shows absent but my child was in school. Why?**
A: Teacher may have made an error. Contact school immediately to correct.

**Q: Can I see other students' information?**
A: No, you can only see your own child's/children's information for privacy.

**Q: What if I disagree with a grade?**
A: Contact the teacher through the portal or schedule a meeting to discuss.

**Q: How far back can I see records?**
A: Usually you can see current and past academic years. Check with school about archives.

**Q: Can I print information from the portal?**
A: Yes, most pages have a print option.

### Payment Questions

**Q: Can I pay fees through the portal?**
A: Depends on school setup. Check portal for online payment option or pay at school office.

**Q: How do I get a receipt for payments?**
A: Download from payment history or request at school office.

**Q: Can I set up payment plan?**
A: Contact school office through portal or in person to arrange payment plan.

**Q: Why does my fee balance seem incorrect?**
A: Contact school office immediately. They can review and correct if needed.

### Communication Questions

**Q: How quickly do teachers respond to messages?**
A: Usually within 24-48 hours during school days. Longer over weekends/holidays.

**Q: Can I message teachers anytime?**
A: You can send messages anytime, but replies come during school hours.

**Q: What if I need to speak to teacher urgently?**
A: For urgent matters, call school office directly instead of using portal.

**Q: Can I message other parents?**
A: Portal doesn't typically allow parent-to-parent messaging. Contact school for parent contact list.
